About Us

Established in 2001, Perfect Paws has the experience and customer service to provide the Plymouth area with most professional pet care services. We treat your pet as our own and it shows. Whether you make regular grooming appointments, or need an immediate de-skunking, we have what it takes to send you and your 4-legged family member home happy. All breeds are welcome, no matter how big or small.

Cats are also welcome!
